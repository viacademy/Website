    • COURSE DESCRIPTION

    Illustrator is a professional vector-based design and drawing program. Adobe Illustrator is the industry-standard vector graphics software, used worldwide by designers of all types who want to create digital graphics, illustrations and typography for all kinds of media: print, web, interactive, video and mobile.


    Our Adobe Illustrator training based on challenges faced in day to day work environments. Start Learning with Vaagai Academy in Chennai Adobe Illustrator Classroom and Online Training Course. At the end of course you will learn to create posters, symbols, logos, patterns, icons, etc.
